Major Achivements in my career

2018

The Beginning of the Journey

  • Discovered a passion for photography through nature and landscape shots.
  • Participated in the college-level “Photography Club” for the first time.
  • Bought first DSLR camera (Canon EOS 200D).
2019

Learning & Early Recognition

  • Completed a certified course in Basics of Digital Photography.
  • Won 1st Prize in “Nature Through My Lens” photo contest at district level
  • Organized first college photo exhibition showcasing 50+ photographs.
2020

Expanding Creativity

  • Started freelance work covering local events and weddings.
  • Got featured in a local magazine for wildlife photography
  • Began experimenting with aerial and drone photography.
2021

Professional Growth

  • Launched personal photography brand/portfolio website.
  • Collaborated with a travel agency for travel photography projects.
  • Won Best Photographer Award at the State Youth Cultural Festival.
2022

Innovation & Recognition

  • Conducted workshops on Portrait and Street Photography for college students.
  • Photos published in National Geographic Your Shot online community.
  • Reached 10K+ followers on Instagram photography page.
2023

Exhibitions & Global Exposure

  • Invited to International Photo Expo 2023 (Chennai).
  • Won 2nd Prize in “Lens Masters India” national photography contest.
  • Started experimenting with AI-based photo enhancement and editing tools.
2024

Mentor & Creator

  • Served as a guest speaker at a photography seminar for beginners.
  • Published first photo storybook titled Frames of Emotion.
  • Started offering online photography tutorials on YouTube.
